Life Can Be Better

Agile — The Way of Doing Things

Life in software development is writing 250 page requirements documents that nobody reads and become irrelevant the minute they are produced, being totally disengaged from the people who actually use the software, working on projects that never deliver anything even after running for 2 years , communicating with siloed teams through email, being told exactly what to do by a manager high up in a hierarchy, its being in fear of your job because of outsourcing.

Or is it?

Imagine a state where the TEAM takes responsibility for what they work on, where conversations replace contracts, where change is a positive part of life, where everybody is willing to help everybody else out, where the delivery of software is of vital importance to ensure success, where features are shipped daily and users are consulted at every step of the way.

Thats how software delivery should be

This session will show you how you could have a better life working in software delivery. And why you should make other feel the same.
