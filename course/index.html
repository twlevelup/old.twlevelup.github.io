LevelUp Course Details

Week 1

Introduction / Agile Fundamentals / Team forming

Welcome to the program! We'll start by showing you how software development works in the real world - what's good, what's bad, and why we're all about agile.

Once we're all grounded, we'll introduce you to the project you'll be working on (spoiler - its a video game), and we'll setup the teams that you'll be delivering in!

Pre-reading

  • The Agile Manifesto
  • Scrum-XP from the trenches
  • Want to run an agile project?

Week 2

Language fundamentals

For those keen on coding we'll show you the ropes for what we will use, and then its time to get your hands dirty. Not a coder? No problem - we'll take you over to the dark side and show you the fundamentals of analysis, design and project management.

Pre-reading

  • Lua
  • LOVE - The game engine
  • Busted - The Unit Test Framework
  • Lua for Programmers series
  • A short Video about Test Driven Development
  • Lua Missions

Week 3

Testing fundamentals and writing user stories

Quality software is about more than just great code. From unit testing to user testing, we'll teach you the value of a test first philosophy, and show you how to put it into practice. Not a tester? Yes you are! Ensuring quality is everyone's responsibility.

Pre-reading

  • James Bach - Software Testing
  • User Stories Applied
  • Specification by Example

Week 4

Features, Features, Features

This week will be focussing solely on the analysis and delivery of features. Those who wanna code, will code. Those who don't, will help define the stories to your prioritised epics and help to setup the wall to allow the teams visibility of your project. Don't forget the standups and retrospectives too!

Pre-reading

  • What's in a Story?
  • Agile Retrospectives - Ideas for exercises

Week 5

UX & Rapid Design

Halfway! This week we will be focussing on how to make things that user's will fine easy to work with. Plus for those with a graphical and artistic interest some expert help creating images for the games.

Pre-reading

  • Nordstrom - Innovation Lab

Week 6

Project work

3rd week of Project Work. But not only that it is important that whatever students want to know they ask!!!

Pre-reading

  • Jez Humble - Continuous Delivery
  • Martin Fowler - Continuous Integration

Week 7

Project work

4th week of delivery with continued focus on completing features. Starting to realise time is running out and that we need to make sure the most valuable and important features are released.

Pre-reading

  • Prioritisation of Backlogs
  • What is a product Backlog?

Week 8

Project work

Final week for delivery. Take that chance to do something you havent done before in the project team. And that last minute rush to ensure things are done.

Pre-reading

  • Pride & Paradev - A chance to be whatever you want to be

Week 9

Showcase / Retro

Done? Lets see some working software! Time to show off what we've delivered, and then look back to learn from the journey. What have we learned on this project that we could use to do better next time? After that, we'll cap off the semester in style with some social drinks, and celebrate your first software project!

  • Showcase
  • Guidelines for showcases
